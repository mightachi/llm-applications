# Discovery Call Script — GST/ITC Copilot

Goal: validate pain, quantify ITC leakage and time lost, test pricing, recruit design partners.
Keep it to 20–25 minutes. Listen more than you pitch. Do NOT demo until the "Pitch test" section.

---

## A. Warm-up (2 min)
- "Tell me about your business — turnover band, how many suppliers, how many purchase invoices a month?"
- "Who handles GST filing today — in-house, a CA, or both?"

## B. Current process (5 min)
- "Walk me through how you reconcile your purchases against `GSTR-2B` each month."
- "What tools do you use today (Tally, Excel, ClearTax, Zoho...)?"
- "How long does that reconciliation take you / your accountant each month?"  → record **hours/month**
- "What happens when a supplier hasn't filed or the numbers don't match?"

## C. Pain & cost (6 min)
- "Have you ever lost Input Tax Credit because of a mismatch or a supplier not filing?"
- "Roughly how much ITC do you think you lost last year?"  → record **₹ leakage/year**
- "Have you received GSTN notices or penalties for mismatches?"
- "On a scale of 1–10, how painful is monthly GST reconciliation?"  → record **pain score**

## D. Solution reaction (4 min)
- "If a tool could auto-pull your `GSTR-2B`, match every invoice, and show you exactly which ITC
  you're about to lose — plus draft the supplier follow-up in Hindi — how useful would that be?"
- "Would you want it to also prepare a review-ready `GSTR-3B` summary?"

## E. Pricing test (3 min)
- "We're thinking ₹999–₹2,499/month depending on invoice volume, with a free one-time leakage audit first.
  Does that feel fair given what you might recover?"  → record **WTP**
- "Who would need to approve buying this — you, your CA, your finance head?"

## F. Pitch test / close (2 min)
- "Would you be open to being a design partner — we run a free audit on your last quarter's data,
  you give feedback, and you get the tool free during the pilot?"  → record **commit Y/N**
- Get a referral: "Which other business owner or CA should I talk to?"

## Go / no-go thresholds
- PROCEED if: ≥ 6/10 report > ₹10k/month leakage AND ≥ 4/10 commit to a paid pilot.
- RETHINK if: pain scores cluster < 5, or current tools already solve it for < ₹1,000/mo.
