# Funding Action Checklist — Prototype to Scale

The ladder: **self-funded ₹2 lakh prototype → SISFS grant (≤₹20L) → Mudra / CGTMSE for go-to-market.**
All figures verified for 2026; always confirm current numbers on the official portals before applying.

---

## Stage 0 — Foundations (Week 0–2)

- [ ] **Incorporate** a Private Limited or LLP (51%+ Indian promoter shareholding — required for SISFS).
      MCA SPICe+; budget ₹8–15k.
- [ ] **Udyam registration** (free) — MSME status: https://udyamregistration.gov.in
- [ ] **DPIIT recognition** (free, ~2 weeks) — unlocks SISFS, CGTMSE startup ceiling, tax benefits.
      Apply at https://www.startupindia.gov.in (Startup recognition).
- [ ] Open a current account; keep all prototype spend traceable (clean books matter for grant audits).
- [ ] Register the product domain + a one-page landing site with the free ITC-audit lead capture.

## Stage 1 — Prototype (the ₹2 lakh, Week 1–6)

- [ ] Ship the working demo in this repo (reconciliation + copilot + dashboard). DONE as scaffold.
- [ ] Run 8–10 discovery calls (see `research/discovery_call_script.md`); log leakage numbers.
- [ ] Sign 3–5 design partners; capture (anonymised) real GSTR-2B data for a credible demo.
- [ ] Record a 3-minute demo video + screenshots for the deck (Slide 5).

Indicative ₹2 lakh spend: incorporation+DPIIT ₹12–18k · cloud ₹40–50k · LLM/OCR API ₹30–40k ·
GSP sandbox ₹20–30k · domain/landing/branding ₹15k · design-partner incentives ₹20k · buffer ₹20–25k.

## Stage 2 — SISFS grant (Week 4–10, in parallel)

- [ ] Confirm eligibility: DPIIT-recognised, incorporated < 2 years, < ₹10L prior govt support.
- [ ] Shortlist up to **3 incubators** (you can apply to all three simultaneously). Criteria below.
- [ ] Prepare the SISFS application: problem, prototype, milestones, use-of-funds, team, traction.
- [ ] Map the grant to **milestones** (prototype → product testing → market-ready) — disbursed milestone-wise.
- [ ] Apply at https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in (year-round call for applications).

**SISFS terms:** up to **₹20 lakh grant** (PoC/prototype/trials, milestone-based) + up to **₹50 lakh**
debt/convertible for market entry. Sectors preferred include agriculture, financial inclusion, fintech.

### Incubator shortlist criteria
- Operational ≥ 2 years, SISFS-empanelled, active fintech/SaaS portfolio.
- Strong CA/finance ecosystem and MSME access (for design partners + distribution).
- Examples to evaluate (verify empanelment + fit): IIT/IIM incubators (e.g. CIIE.CO at IIM-A,
  SINE at IIT-B, IITM Incubation Cell), T-Hub (Hyderabad), KIIT-TBI, NSRCEL.
- [ ] Email 2–3 incubator managers with a 1-pager before formally applying.

## Stage 3 — Go-to-market debt (post-grant)

- [ ] **PM Mudra (Tarun / Tarun Plus)**: up to **₹20 lakh collateral-free** via JanSamarth/any bank.
      https://www.jansamarth.in / Udyami Mitra.
- [ ] **CGTMSE-backed working capital**: collateral-free guarantee up to **₹10 crore**
      (**₹20 crore** for DPIIT-recognised startups) through a member lending bank (CGTMSE only guarantees,
      it does not lend — approach an MLI bank).
- [ ] Keep a clean Udyam, a real project report, and a banker who can defend the file.

## Stage 4 — Beyond (optional)

- [ ] State startup-policy grants (Maharashtra/Gujarat) — stackable, but stay under the ₹10L
      prior-govt-support cap *before* SISFS.
- [ ] Angel/seed once you have ARR; the govt grant + working prototype de-risks the round.

| Scheme | What | Limit | Key condition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| SISFS debt | Market entry | ≤ ₹50 lakh | Convertible/debt via incubator |
| PM Mudra (Tarun+) | Working capital | ≤ ₹20 lakh | Collateral-free, micro/small unit |
